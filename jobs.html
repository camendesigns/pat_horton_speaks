Job Creation Platform

Educating the residence of Cook County is critical to the implementation of services that are available to the community through the county government. It is when our public becomes highly educated with the resources available to them that we are able to assist those who are seeking Cook County government services including jobs. Cook County currently employs 22,000 individuals and own about 954 square miles of land, making them a strong partner in the continual development of our community.

As the Business Development Director for Deb Corp North (Rogers Park Chambers of Commerce), my project was the development of the Gateway Project in which we trained and hired about 150 individuals from the Rogers Park Community. Building an alliance with the community, business leaders, property owners, and chambers will allow us to continue business development in District 3. Thereby establishing a robust economy, economic growth, and job creation.
